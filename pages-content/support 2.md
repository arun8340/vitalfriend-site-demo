## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
### Getting Started
**How do I create a VitalFriend account?**
Your account is typically created by your healthcare provider or care organization. Once enrolled, you’ll receive an email or SMS with instructions to download the app and log in.
**Which devices does VitalFriend support?**
VitalFriend supports FDA-cleared remote monitoring devices including blood pressure monitors, pulse oximeters, weight scales, and other connected health devices, depending on your care plan.
### Using the App
**How do I add or pair a device?**
Open the VitalFriend mobile app, go to *Devices*, and follow the step-by-step pairing instructions. Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone.
**What if my vitals are not syncing?**
Check that Bluetooth is turned on, your device is charged, and the app is up to date. If the issue continues, contact support.
**Can caregivers or family members access my data?**
Yes. With your consent, caregivers and family members can be granted limited access to view vitals and receive alerts.
### Data, Privacy & Security
**Is my health data secure?**
Yes. VitalFriend is HIPAA-compliant and uses industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and health information.
**Who can see my health data?**
Only you, authorized caregivers, and your healthcare team can access your data, based on permissions you approve.
## Troubleshooting
- Ensure your app is updated to the latest version
- Restart the app or your device
- Check internet or cellular connectivity
- Re-pair your monitoring device if needed
If problems persist, please contact our support team.
